# Introduction to JSDocs



## Thoughts

Tags:
  * @type
  * @param
  * @returns

Types:
  * primitives (string, number, null, undefined, etc.)
  * objects ({a: string, b: number})
  * optionals (string?)
  * functions (function(string): number)
  * disjoint unions (string | boolean)
  * arrays (string[])
  * maps ({[key: string]: number})
